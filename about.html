ABOUT US

Incorporated in May 1986, Pioneer Property Management Ltd is a one-stop consultancy shop for all your real estate needs. With over three-and-a-half decades of experience in putting a smile on the face of our clientele, at Pioneer, we have always made total transparency our calling card, and ‘Building Trust and Creating Value’ our credo. Flagged off by Rajkumar Khaitan and Ravindra Khaitan, Pioneer Property Management Ltd gathered further steam under the dynamic stewardship of the youngest brother Jitendra Khaitan, who came on board in 1992 and infused new momentum in Pioneer with his sharp, out-of-the-box ideas, totally in sync with today’s market demands.

Our Core Management Team:

At present, under the table tutelage of Jitendra Khaitan and his brother-in-law Ramesh Kejriwal, the millennial generation led by Yash Vardhan Kejriwal, and ably aided by Ayush Khaitan, is taking Pioneer Property Management to new heights, mixing the invaluable knowledge absorbed from the old guard and using their own smarts and contemporary, cutting-edge skillsets, to cater to the ever-changing needs and aspirations of the modern consumer. The goal is to expand operations and set new benchmarks in the domain of organized real estate consultancy across the country.
